Spacedog is learning how to drive a space truck.
Spacedog is very bad at driving a spacetruck.
This is because Spacedog is a dog.

How to play:

(dog is optional)

Spacedog! is an exhibition only Oculus VR game controlled by an AKAI LPD8 where you have to save your truck from intersteller destruction by responding to a myriad of absurd minigames.

It's like Spaceteam crossed with Warioware

where you can't see your hands
